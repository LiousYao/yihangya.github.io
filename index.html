Hi, welcome to my website! I am a second-year Ph.D. candidate in Safe AI Lab at Carnegie Mellon University, advised by Prof. Ding Zhao. I also work closely with Dr. Tingnan Zhang and Dr. Wenhao Yu at Google DeepMind. Before CMU, I received my Bachelor's degree from Zhiyuan Honor College, Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU) in 2022 fortunately advised by Prof. Wei Dong at SJTU RI. In 2021, I spent a wonderful time working as a research intern in the Intelligent Control Lab led by Prof. Changliu Liu at CMU.

My research topic focuses on reinforcement learning and its application in autonomous systems. I am especially interested in improving safety, i.e., constraint satisfaction, and sampling efficiency for the reinforcement learning methods.
